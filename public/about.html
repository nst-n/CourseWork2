About Us



Our Mission

The Scottish Pantry Network (TSPN) is dedicated to addressing the growing issue of food insecurity in our communities. With the cost of living crisis on the rise, more and more people are struggling to access affordable and nutritious food. TSPN collaborates with local food pantries to provide vital support to those in need.


Our Initiative

We understand the importance of community-led efforts in combating food insecurity. In response to initiatives by Glasgow City Council to support local food production, TSPN is leveraging technology to connect individuals with surplus fruits and vegetables to nearby food pantries. Our web application aims to streamline the process of food donation and distribution, starting with a pilot program in Glasgow.


Making a Difference

Together, we can make a meaningful impact on food insecurity in our communities. Join TSPN in our mission to ensure that no one goes hungry.
